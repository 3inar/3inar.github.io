## Teaching
NTNU has a [conversion from a 100 point scale to letter grade](https://i.ntnu.no/wiki/-/wiki/norsk/prosentvurderingsmetoden) as follows:
```
A: 89–100 
B: 77–88
C: 65–76
D: 53–64
E: 41–52
F: 0–40
```
