Bits of string: compositional data

(§) The article A flexible Bayesian tool for CoDa mixed models (Martı́nez-Minaya and Rue 2024) looks promising; it frames compositional count data in a way so that it’s possible to fit by using INLA.

(§) Dimension of of centered log ratio vectors matters. Compare (−1,1,0)(-1, 1, 0) with (−1,1,0,0,…)(-1, 1, 0, 0, \ldots) (a hundred zeros repeated). The former is roughly (0.09,0.67,0.24)(0.09, 0.67, 0.24) in the simplex, while the latter is roughly (0.004,0.03,0.01,0.01,…)(0.004, 0.03, 0.01, 0.01, \ldots), which is practically uniform. The log ratios between coordinates 1 and 2 (or 1 and 3) remain the same however.
